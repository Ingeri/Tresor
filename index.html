Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer international shipping through DHL with real-time tracking. Customers can track their orders through a provided tracking number after dispatch.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), and other payment options via Pesapal and Flutterwave. We ensure secure transactions for all payments.
Are your paintings hand-made?
All our paintings are hand-painted by professional artists using high-quality materials. We ensure that every piece is unique and meets the highest standards of craftsmanship.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times depend on your location. Local deliveries take 2-5 business days, while international deliveries may take 7-14 business days.
Do you offer custom paintings?
Yes, we offer custom paintings based on your preferences. You can provide reference images, colors, and dimensions to get a unique piece.
What materials do you use for your paintings?
We use high-quality acrylic and oil paints on premium canvas. Our materials ensure long-lasting durability and vibrant colors.
Can I return or exchange a painting?
We accept returns or exchanges within 7 days of receiving your order. The painting must be in its original condition. Return shipping costs apply.
How can I take care of my painting?
Avoid direct sunlight and moisture. Clean gently with a dry, soft cloth. Do not use cleaning liquids or chemicals.
Do you offer framing services?
Yes, we offer framing options for all paintings. You can select a frame that suits your decor during checkout.
